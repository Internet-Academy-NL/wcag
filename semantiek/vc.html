Wake up! How to use Voice Control

Intro

  • StarTrek

  • Dragon, professional voice recognition software

Turn on Dictation: On your Mac, choose Apple menu > System Settings, then click Accessibility in the sidebar. You may need to scroll down. Go to Voice Control on the right, then turn it on. If a prompt appears, click Enable.
